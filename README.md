### The Rules of Engagement (There Are None)

1. **Everything works, but nothing makes sense.**
   - Forms accept input but may reject it later.
   - Buttons lead places they're not labeled to lead.
   - Pages contain information that contradicts other pages.

2. **The pirate captain is always watching.**
   - He occasionally blocks important UI elements.
   - When he does, he glows with cyan neon.
   - He has no effect on actual functionality, but his presence be felt.

3. **Cyan text is deceptive.**
   - Important warnings be written in cyan.
   - These warnings be the *least* important information.
   - This be me way of keeping ye on yer toes.

4. **Time has no meaning.**
   - "3-5 business storms" may mean anything.
   - "Yesterday" might be next week.
   - "Verification codes expire before arrival" be factually accurate.

5. **Witty people will notice the following:**
   - Some buttons are rotated at odd angles (transformation: rotate)
   - Some fields are placed where they shouldn't be (overflow: hidden, but scrollable)
   - Some sections contain false information labeled as important
   - Some pages lead to other pages that lead back to the same page
   - Some navigation options appear disabled but are actually clickable
